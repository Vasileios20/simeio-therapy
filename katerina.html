Katerina Gesou

Katerina Gesou is a graduate of the BSc Occupational Therapy, Queen Margaret University and the Department of Physical Education and Sports Science (TEFAA) specializing in Special Physical Education. She continued her specialization with two master's degrees. The first comes from the Postgraduate Program "Kinesiology" of the Department of Physical Education and Sport Science of Serres where she dealt with the effect of Electrical Stimulation on the fatigue of the muscles of the thenar while the second from the Special Inclusive Education European University of Cyprus where she dealt with the Evaluation of the understanding of the emotions of others through Psychomotor by children with Autism Spectrum Disorder. At the level of training, she has completed the training of Sensory Integration while her personal portfolio includes the Seminar on learning infant swimming, the seminar on Psychomotor- Music-Kinetic Education of EKPA and the seminar of the Hellenic Society Halliwic. Swimming and Therapy for People with Special needs. She has also attended the 216-hour Professional Yoga Learning Program specializing in children's yoga. She is specialized in PECS and TEEACH methods, Social Histories, Behavioral Management methods for people with disabilities, Motor Learning and Cognitive Orientation Approach in the daily execution of a CO-OP (Cognitive Orientation to daily Occupational Performance) project. From 2005 to 2018 she worked as an external collaborator in kindergartens and structures for people with disabilities, organizing psychomotor and adapted physical activity programs for children with difficulties in movement. Since 2007 she has been delivering personalized programs of Kinetic Intervention, Occupational Therapy and Hydrotherapy at home in collaboration with private and public swimming pools of the city and from 2012-2017 she provided educational parallel support to children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) and Down syndrome, while at the same time she was an external partner in a private Physiotherapy Center where she began to specialize in pediatric Occupational Therapy. From 2017-2022 she worked in large Rehabilitation Centers framing the Department of Adult Occupational Therapy, while at the same time she was in charge of their respective Pediatric Department. Since 2021 she has been working as an occupational therapist at the Pediatric Therapy Center "Excelixi" of Elena Koundourou while also collaborating with the Metropolitan College of Thessaloniki as a teacher. The collaboration with Elena Koundourou developed and matured, resulting in the creation of the Center for Special Therapies "Simeio". From the beginning of her career until now she has not stopped training, while her unstoppable passion and love for Occupational Therapy remain the driving force to continue her Lifelong Education.
